End-User License Agreement

Application name: BPS QBO Automation

Last updated: July 6, 2026

1. Purpose of the Application

BPS QBO Automation is an internal automation tool used by BPS to connect with authorized QuickBooks Online companies and perform accounting support processes, including updating exchange rate information, reviewing chart of accounts activity, validating parent account postings, reviewing accounting close dates, generating internal reports, and sending related notifications.

2. Authorized Use

This application is intended only for use by authorized BPS personnel and authorized QuickBooks Online company administrators. The application may only be connected to QuickBooks Online companies for which proper authorization has been granted.

3. Access to QuickBooks Online Data

By connecting a QuickBooks Online company to this application, the authorized user allows the application to access the QuickBooks Online data necessary to perform the enabled automation processes. This may include company information, chart of accounts data, accounting reports, company preferences, exchange rate information, and other accounting-related data required for the operation of the tool.

4. Internal Business Use

The application is provided for internal business and accounting process automation. It is not intended for resale, public distribution, or use by unauthorized third parties.

5. User Responsibilities

Users are responsible for ensuring that they have the authority to connect a QuickBooks Online company to the application. Users must not use the application to access, process, or modify data without proper authorization.

6. Data Accuracy

The application is designed to support accounting review and automation processes. Users remain responsible for reviewing reports, validations, accounting outputs, and any results generated by the application before relying on them for accounting, tax, financial reporting, or operational decisions.

7. Third-Party Services

The application connects to QuickBooks Online through Intuit APIs. Use of QuickBooks Online remains subject to Intuit's own terms, policies, permissions, and platform availability.

8. Termination of Access

BPS may revoke or disable access to the application at any time. A connected QuickBooks Online company may also be disconnected by revoking the application's access from within QuickBooks Online or Intuit account settings.

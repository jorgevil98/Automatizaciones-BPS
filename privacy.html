Privacy Policy

Application name: BPS QBO Automation

Last updated: July 6, 2026

1. Overview

This Privacy Policy explains how BPS QBO Automation collects, uses, stores, and protects data when connected to authorized QuickBooks Online companies.

2. Information We Access

When a QuickBooks Online company is connected to the application, the application may access accounting and company data necessary to perform authorized automation processes. This may include:

  • QuickBooks Online company identifier, also known as the realm ID;
  • Company information and preferences;
  • Chart of accounts data;
  • Accounting reports and account balances;
  • Transaction or report data required to validate account activity;
  • Accounting close date or related company preference information;
  • Exchange rate information, when applicable;
  • Execution logs, validation results, and internal report outputs.

3. How We Use the Information

The information accessed by the application is used only for authorized accounting automation and control processes, including:

  • Updating or validating exchange rate information;
  • Reviewing whether parent accounts have direct accounting activity;
  • Reviewing accounting close date information;
  • Generating internal accounting control reports;
  • Sending internal notifications or exception reports to authorized personnel;
  • Maintaining logs for monitoring, troubleshooting, and audit support.

4. OAuth Tokens and Credentials

The application uses OAuth tokens provided through Intuit's authorization process to connect to QuickBooks Online. These tokens are used only to access authorized QuickBooks Online companies and are stored securely for operational purposes. The application does not collect or store QuickBooks Online user passwords.

5. Data Storage

Data required for the operation of the application may be stored in internal systems controlled by BPS, including configuration databases, logs, and report output folders. Stored data may include company identifiers, connection status, automation results, error logs, and report files generated by the application.

6. Data Sharing

BPS does not sell QuickBooks Online data accessed through this application. Data is not shared with third parties except when required to operate the application, comply with legal obligations, support authorized internal business processes, or as otherwise authorized by the connected company.

7. Data Security

BPS applies reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational safeguards to protect data accessed and processed by the application. Access to the application, stored tokens, reports, and logs is restricted to authorized personnel.

8. Data Retention

Data is retained only for as long as necessary to support the application's business, accounting, operational, audit, or compliance purposes. Retention periods may vary depending on internal policies, legal requirements, and the nature of the generated reports or logs.

9. Disconnecting the Application

Access to a QuickBooks Online company may be revoked by disconnecting the application from Intuit or QuickBooks Online account settings. After disconnection, the application will no longer be able to access that QuickBooks Online company unless authorization is granted again.

10. User Control and Requests

Authorized users or company administrators may contact BPS to request information about the application's connection status, data handling, or disconnection process.

11. Changes to This Policy

BPS may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in the application, operational processes, legal requirements, or security practices. The updated version will be published on this page.

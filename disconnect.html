Disconnect

Access to BPS QBO Automation can be disconnected by revoking the application's access from the connected Intuit or QuickBooks Online account settings.

Once disconnected, BPS QBO Automation will no longer be able to access the QuickBooks Online company unless access is authorized again.

If you need assistance disconnecting a QuickBooks Online company from this application, please contact BPS support.
